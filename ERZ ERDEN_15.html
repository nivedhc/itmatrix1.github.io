ERZ ERDEN'15

"ERZ ERDEN'15" was a National level IT Fest

"ERZ ERDEN'15"was an organized by Computer Science department on behalf of BCA Association IT-MATRIX on 9th and 10th september 2015:10 AM It was an program based on students personal skills on computer knowledge.

The Inaugural ceremony was conducted at college auditorium.Welcome speech was presented by Mrs.Reshma Thoonoli (H.O.D)department of computer sceince,Mr.prof P Moosa (Principle of itm college) delivered thier presidential address,textial vote proposed by Mrs.Nishitha M (teacher coordinator).Dr.Babu Anto P (IT Dept,Kannur University) inaugrated the function and gave energy to all students and he explained about the importance of computer technics and also apprecaite Computer sceince department to conducted such an event.The chairman Mr.Muneer.K.K,Hira Charitable Trust who as graciously accepected our invitation to join with the fest. Dr.Veeran Kutty (Principle,ITM mayyil),T.V Hasainar Master (Hira charitable Trust),Mr.Abdul Razak (Secretary,Hira Charitable Trust), Mr.Abubakker P.M (Principle,ITM Public School),prof.PankajaKshan(Commerce HOD),Mr.Ramanujan V.A (Administrator),Mr.Balakrishnan K.P (PTA Representative), Mr.Muhammed Jowhar (CDO),Mr.Jithesh P (Staff Advisor),Mrs.Preethi (Staff Secretary),Mr.Grishil (Union Chairman) was delivered their felicitation address. Nizamudhin M.M (student coordinator) was delivered the Vote of Thanks.

The program regitration has been started at 9am - 10am.Opening ceremony was started 10am- 11am.The main aim of the program was helping the students skills and talent. the main events are Triefen(coding) 1st prize:7000 and 2nd prize: 3000, Ozean(Web Designing)1st prize:5000 and 2nd prize:3000,Terrain(IT Quiz) 1st prize:7000 and 2nd prize:3000, Forsten(Treasure Hunt) 1st prize:10,000 and 2nd prize:5000 ,Erdreich(gaming) 1st prize:7000 and 2nd prize:3000,Auftreten(photography) prize:4000,
