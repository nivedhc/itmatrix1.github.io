WEBINAR REPORT

"BASICS OF PYTHON WITH DATA SCIENCE"

Date:23/09/2020

The department of BCA in association with IQAC conducted the webinar on "BASICS OF PYTHON WITH DATA SCIENCE" on 23/09/2020 for the students and teaching faculties of Kannur University.There were totally 101 participants, out of which 60 participants from ITM Collage of Arts and Science,Mayyil and 41 participants from various other institution. MS Dhanya G, "Rathinam Collage of Arts and Science, Coimbatore"delivered the lecture.

The impact of Python with Data Science in recent techonology was discussed in this session. The speaker presented the basic concepts and show python installation. She also executed some examples practically.

The session was started at 10 am. Mrs. Reshma Thoonoli (HOD BCA department)delivered welcome speech. Registration link was created using Google form and the program conducted through Google meet. Mr Jithesh P(Asst.professor BCA department and program coordinator) delivered vote of thanks at the end. The feedback about this sessions that will conduct in the future and all the participants received E-Certificate.
