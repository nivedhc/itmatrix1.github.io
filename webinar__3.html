Webinar 3: Academic Projects and IT's Importance

" Academic Projects and IT's Importance" was an extension activity organized by Computer Science department on 20-08-2020 from 10AM to 11:30AM.
ITM College of arts and science,Department of computer Application conducted a webinar from 10AM to 11:30AM on 20-08-2020 on the topic "Academic Projects and IT's Importance". The webinar was handled by Mr.Likhil.T project Manager RISS Technologies.The class, attended by 50 students,was held on Google Meet. The students commented that the class was very useful and informative.

The main aim of the program was to make people aware of the importance of IT and the help that Information Technology provides to the young minds to create their own projects for academic purpose and also for their future carrier accomplishments.
