PRIMOR-2K18

"PRIMOR-2K18" is a two day intra-collegiate IT fest

"PRIMOR-2K18"is a two day intra-collegiate IT fest conducted by the Computer Science Department of ITM College of Arts and Science College,Mayyil,Kannur.
The fest was held from 31st July to 1st August.

On 27th July 2018,Logo promotion had been conductwd in the Seminar hall at 10 am.Mr.Muneer K.K.(Chairman ITM College of Arts and Science College) was the Guest of Honour at the function.Mrs.Nishitha M(Teacher coordinator) delivered the welcome note.Principal Mr.Prof.P.Moosa made students aware of the importance of the fest and showered his blessings through his Presidential address.The session followed by the launch of Logo by the chief guest.The felicitation address was delivered by Mr.Jithesh P.(Asst.Prof,CS Department)and Mrs.Bindu(Asst.Prof,Malayalam Department).Mr.Mohammed Nihal Masheer(Student coordinator) delivered the Vote of Thanks.

More than 150 students from different departments registered for the fest.The fest included many evente such as IT-Quiz,Web Designing,Gaming,Treasure Hunt and Photography. The fest also included many Spot events as well. In addition a grand Dance competition was conducted. The closing ceremony was conducted around 3:30 pm.Cash prizes and Mementos were awarded for the winners by the Chairman and the Principal.The function concluded with the Vote of Thanks delivered by Mrs.Reshma T.(HOD,CS,Department).
