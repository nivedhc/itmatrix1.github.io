ZMAGA

"ZMAGA" was an intra collegiate it fest

"ZMAGA"was an it fest organized by Computer Science department on behalf of BCA Association IT-MATRIX on 14th feb 2023. It was an it fest based program .This activity was mainly conducted for the students in the college . The participation extended over 100 students.

The main aim of the it fest program was helping students unleashed their skills and talents.it also provide opportunities with their peers and form new connection. The whole program was controlled by All the students and staffs of BCA department.

The program has been conducted in the New block at 9:30am.It started with a prayer . The Welcome speech proposed by Mrs. Reshma Thoonoli(hod, dept of computer science). Presidential address was given by Mrs.Ramya E.B(Principal in-charge,hod commerce) Mr.Muneerk.k was the chief Guest of the Inaugural programme (Chairman,Hira charitable trust).key note address was delivered by Mr.Jithesh p (Program co-ordinator,Asst.prof.dept of computer science). Felicitation speech given by Mr. Ratheesh T(Staff Secretary),Dr.Rosy Antony(IQAC Co-ordinator,hod chemistry).Vote of thanks delivered by Ms.Anamika Pramod(Student co-ordinator). The main events are Arean(computer games)Event1 rs. 2250,Event2 rs.1500,Jumanji(treasure hunt)1st rs.1500,2nd rs.1000,Arcus(digital poster designing)1st rs.750,2nd rs.500 Nuvo(dance)1st rs.2000,2nd rs.1000 and other sport event like Ring throw,Cup pyramid etc.
