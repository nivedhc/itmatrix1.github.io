PROJECT

"Guest talk " on Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

"Guest talk" ITM College of Arts and Science in association with IT Matrix organized a 'Guest talk on Data Science and Artificial Intelligence on 3 November 2022 at 2pm in the college seminar hall. Dr Jithesh K, Associate professor department of C.S.- MG college, Iritty and Academic Council member of Kannur University was the chief guest of the day. The speaker highlighted the significance of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence in the present scenario. He also discussed about the various fields on the same.

The program began with a welcome address by Mrs. Reshma Thoonoli, H.O.D-B.C.A.department followed by the Presidential address of Prof. Majeed P.P., Principal- ITM College of Arts and Science , Pro. Muneer K.K., delivered the chairperson's address . Felicitations to the program was offered by Mr.T.Ratheesh (Staff Secretary) and Mr.Jithesh P.(Staff Advisor).Ms Anamika Pramod, BCA Association Secretary ,delivered the vote of thanks.
